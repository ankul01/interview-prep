# Behavioral Interview Preparation

Structured stories and frameworks for behavioral rounds.

## Categories
- **Leadership** — Leading teams, driving vision, influencing without authority
- **Conflict Resolution** — Handling disagreements, cross-team friction
- **Failure Stories** — What went wrong, lessons learned, course corrections
- **Scaling Systems** — Scaling teams, processes, and technology
- **Stakeholder Management** — Aligning with PMs, executives, and cross-functional partners
- **Decision Making** — How you evaluate tradeoffs and make tough calls
- **Ownership** — End-to-end accountability, going beyond scope
